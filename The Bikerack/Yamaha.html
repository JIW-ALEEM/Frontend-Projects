YAMAHA

1.Yamaha MT-10:

It all points to a stupidfast bike that boasts an MT-10 top speed of 155 mph, up by 3 mph for this model year. It has a blisteringly quick 0-to-130 mph time of only 8.95 seconds. The power flows through a slipper-type clutch.
Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, transverse inline-Four, DOHC w/ 4 valves per cyl.
Displacement: 998cc.
Bore x Stroke: 79.0 x 50.9mm.
Horsepower: 138 hp at 9,400 rpm (rear-wheel dyno, previous model)





2.Yamaha MT-09:

Yamaha 2022 MT-09 is powered by 889 cc engine. This 2021 MT-09 engine generates a power of 119 PS @ 10000 rpm and a torque of 93 Nm @ 7000 rpm. Yamaha 2021 MT-09 gets Disc brakes in the front and rear. Yamaha 2021 MT-09 has Tubeless Tyre and Alloy Wheels.
Engine Type: 890cc, liquid-cooled DOHC inline 3-cylinder; 16 valves.
Horsepower: 117 HP.
Torque: 69 lb-ft.
Wet Weight: 417 lbs (189 kg)
Seat Height: 32.5 inches (825.5 mm)





3.Yamaha YZF-R6:

YZF-R1 is Yamaha's fastest, quickest and most powerful Superbike ever. The design is inspired by Yamaha's MotoGP race bike YZR-M1 with an air-intake at the middle of the front fairing. The same goes for the engine, the inline 4 unit which is also a direct lift from M1's engine architecture.
Maximum Torque: 61.7 Nm @ 10500 rpm
Number of Cylinders: 4
Number of Gears: 6
Engine cc (Displacement): 599 cc Maximum Power: 118.4 HP @ 14,500 rpm Seat Height: 850 mm Kerb Weight: 190 kg
